Methodology & Assumptions

Words processed: 1.4M total delivered; ~90% (1.26M) evaluated by AI tool; remainder includes manually handled strings.

Segment size: Average 20 words/segment estimated from typical Amazon product description content structure.

System prompt tokens (~15,000): Back-calculated from AWS billing data. Incorporates comprehensive NB-NO QA rules across 7 feedback rounds.

Bedrock pricing: Cross-region inference (us.anthropic.*) model IDs. Exact per-token rate varies by model; costs shown are AWS actual figures.

Human QA rate: $40/hr (NO-NB linguistic rate as reported). 2,000 words/hr standard throughput. PM overhead assumed at +20%.

July remaining forecast: AWS Cost Management projection ($18,363.55 full month). Actual spend may vary depending on batch volumes.

Sonnet savings ratio: Sonnet 4.6 priced at 60% of Opus 4.7 per million tokens (input: $3 vs $5; output: $15 vs $25).

Prompt caching: Not enabled during Opus period. Enabling cache on the system prompt for Sonnet batches could reduce input costs by an additional 50–90%.

Human QA monthly split: ~300K words attributed to June, ~1.1M to July, based on observed billing ramp-up pattern.

Quality note: Zero client complaints as of July 22, 2026. No rework cycles have been initiated. This record validates both tool quality and the Language Lead feedback integration process.
